\subsection*{Ingredients}

\begin{itemize}
  \item $ 4 $ chicken breasts, skinless
  \item $ 2 $ tablespoon harissa
  \item $ 1 $ teaspoon olive oil
  \item $ 1 $ teaspoon dried oregano
  \item $ 6 $ large olives, halved
  \item $ \qty{250}{\gram} $ cherry tomatoes
\end{itemize}

\medskip

\subsection*{Method}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Put chicken, run with the harissa, oil, oregano and vodka. Marinade for an hour or more.
  \item Heat oven to \qty{200}{\celsius} fan.
  \item Pan fry the chicken until sealed.
  \item Add the cherry tomatoes and olives to the tray. Roast until the tomato skins start to split and the chicken is cooked through.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Harissa}

If you don't have harissa then prepare this alternative:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item tomato paste, smoked paprika, and Moroccan chili powder with \textbf{Berbere Spice Blend}
\end{enumerate}
