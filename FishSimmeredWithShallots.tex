\subsection*{Ingredients}

\begin{itemize}
\item 1 teaspoon peanut oil
\item 2 cloves garlic, crushed
\item 4 green shallots, chopped
\item 2 tablespoons brown sugar
\item $ \frac {1}{3} $ cup ($\qty{80}{\ml}$) fish sauce
\item $ \frac {2}{3} $ cup ($\qty{160}{\ml}$) water
\item 8 fish cutlets
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Method}

\begin{enumerate}
\item
  Heat oil in a large pan, add garlic and half the shallots, cook,
  stirring, until shallots are soft. Add sugars, sauce and water, stir
  over heat, without boiling, until sugar is dissolved; bring to boil.
\item
  Add fish to pan, simmer, uncovered, until fish is cooked.
\item
  Remove fish from the pan. Keep warm.
\item
  Simmer pan juices, uncovered, until reduced to about $ \frac{1}{3} $ cup
  ($\qty{160}{\ml}$)
\item
  Serve over fish.
\item
  Sprinkle with remaining shallots.
\end{enumerate}

Serves 4.
